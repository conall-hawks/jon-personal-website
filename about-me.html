Taking a Step Back

Some of my best code was revealed to me during a drive, taking a walk, or other activities. Taking a break from the screen and getting some air can help give me a clear perspective. In addition, revisiting my work can reveal a better way; things I hadn't seen before.

I do my best to keep my activities diverse. However--at the end of the day--I'm always writing code.
